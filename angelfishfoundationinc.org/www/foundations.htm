FULFILLING A DREAM
OUR WORK

Years ago a spark of compassion inspired the dream that led to the creation of The AngelFish Foundation, it was that spark that this dream was built upon and evolved into the foundation and the guiding force behind AngelFish Foundation. AFF's motto is simply that children are people and all deserve a bright tomorrow, and the driving force behind the foundation as envisioned.

AFF has been establish to promote awareness, support research and pioneering treatments and possible future cures, provide education for families. It is the vision of the foundation to bring these under one roof by providing a mobile school for children with Autism and Epilepsy where they can feel safe in their own home, provide home health aide services by the best in their field. Today, every dollar donated goes directly to providing/managing the specialized services we provide for children and their families.
MISSION

Our mission is to start a mobile services organization for children and their families with special needs children with a primary focus on Autism, Epilepsy and childhood cancers. On a case by case basis will approve enrollment of children/families with other special needs as well. Our number one focus is our student’s education, safety, health, and environment. We will provide a learning curriculum and experience for special needs children in a fun, safe environment with a tailored curriculum based on the child’s personal needs and developmental growth while providing the expertise/well qualified, experienced Instructors, student aids, therapists in special needs education and mental health.

Mobile services to include but not limited to (Home health aides, Sign Language instructors, motor skill development, academic tutoring, mental health services) Our goal is to acquire proper facilities, staff, and open the mobile services by September 2013 (funding levels will determine staff size and enrollment capabilities). Funding sources will include private grants, business and public donations, and Tuition of which percentage paid will be determined by ability to pay. We will devote 100% of time required to operate, maintain, and meet the needs of our students of which will be directed by licensed, certified, and experienced individuals based on individual responsibilities along with the mindset that the child comes first and conducts themselves according to the mobile services mission.

When available funds permit we will provide financial support for medical research of Autism and Epilepsy. We only provide financial support for other established non-profit organization.
