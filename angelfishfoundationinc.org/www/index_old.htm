Fulfilling a Dream

Some years ago a spark of compassion inspired the dream that led to the creation of The AngelFish Foundation, it was that spark that this dream was built upon the evolved into the foundation and guiding force of The AngelFish Foundation. The logo reflects the Autism puzzle pieces and the world as a whole signifying that children are just people and all deserve a bright tomorrow, and the driving force of the foundation as envisioned.

Located in Hatfield, P.A., the Foundation seeks to fulfill the visionary genius and innovative dream of Its founder. Governed by a board of trustees, foundation staff conduct disbursement of funds according to guidelines set forth in the foundations bylaws.
Our Work

The foundation has been establish to promote awareness, support research, provide education for families. It is the vision of the foundation to bring these under one roof in the near future by providing a school for children with Autism and Epilepsy where they can feel safe and at home, provide medical services by the best specialists in the field that also contribute in the area of research and pioneering treatments and possible cures. Today, more than 85 percent of our total spending goes to funding the specialized services we provide for children and their families.
